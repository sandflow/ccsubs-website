Meet-up @ NAB 2024
Live captioning and subtitling for streaming

Apply today

The CCSUBS group invites you to apply to the following meet-up:

Topic
Interoperability challenges in live captioning and subtitling for streaming
When
April 15, 2024 from 13:00 to 17:00
Where
Las Vegas, NV, USA

The subtitling and captioning industry remains fragmented by technology and practices. This makes it expensive, if not impossible, to deliver great experiences.

This unique event will bring together 30 technology experts from streaming services, studios, browser vendors, and suppliers to explore these challenges. While the primary focus will be on the technology stack for live captioning and subtitling in streaming, there will also be an opportunity for participants to bring additional problem statements for discussion by the group.

The meetup will be organized around presentations, interactive discussions, and a short unconference session. We plan on publishing a report following the event.

There is no registration fee.

The application deadline is February 10, 2024. Space is limited. A response to your application will be provided on February 15, 2024.

Program committee: Pierre-Anthony Lemieux (Sandflow Consulting), Andreas Tai (a2a11y), Eric Carlson (Apple), Cyril Concolato (Netflix), Flavio Ribeiro (Netflix), Ken Harrenstien and Bill McLaughlin.

Please contact the program committee chairs Pierre-Anthony Lemieux and Andreas Tai with any question.
